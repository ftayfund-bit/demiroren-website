Our Mission

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in London, Demirøren Nordic Capital represents the convergence of generational business wisdom and contemporary investment strategy. We are a diversified holding company committed to creating sustainable value across European markets.

"We stand before you as third-generation members and principal shareholders of one of the region's most respected conglomerates. With more than 25 years each in our family business, we have witnessed and contributed to its growth across multiple economic cycles. Today, as we prepare the fourth generation to join this journey, we are building a platform that embodies the principles of the past while forging its own distinct path."

Heritage

Three generations of entrepreneurial leadership with deep roots in European business and proven track record across economic cycles.

Expertise

Over a century of combined operational experience across our leadership team in building and scaling businesses across multiple industries and geographies.

Vision

Creating a sustainable investment platform for the next generation while maintaining the values of integrity, excellence, and long-term thinking.

Our Portfolio

We have built a diversified platform spanning four complementary pillars, each operating independently while creating synergistic value for our stakeholders.

YIELD FUND

Velorin

A sophisticated credit platform delivering consistent returns through diversified investments in senior real estate debt, infrastructure bonds, and investment-grade credit opportunities across European markets.

INSURANCE PLATFORM

Norven

Building a next-generation European insurance platform with comprehensive coverage across the United Kingdom, Germany, Nordics, and Benelux, serving individuals and businesses with tailored solutions.

REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT

Tierra Fina

Premium European real estate development focused on institutional-grade residential assets including build-to-rent, student housing, and mixed-use projects in strategic German and UK locations.

SUSTAINABLE INFRASTRUCTURE

Norclime

Investing in the transition to a sustainable economy through renewable energy, clean technology, sustainable transport, and circular economy infrastructure projects across Europe.

Leadership
