Governance & Oversight

Comprehensive institutional framework designed to protect investor interests through independent oversight, regulatory compliance, and transparent operations. Multi-layered governance structure ensures accountability at every level of fund management and asset operations.

Fund Structure

Luxembourg SCSp vehicle under CSSF supervision. Independent fund administrator managing NAV calculations, investor reporting, and regulatory compliance. Segregated custody through tier-1 banking partner.

Independent Oversight

Big Four annual audit with quarterly reviews. Independent valuation agent providing property appraisals semi-annually. Advisory board comprising institutional investors with governance and veto rights over key decisions.

Reporting & Transparency

Monthly asset-level performance reports. Quarterly financial statements with detailed variance analysis. Annual investor meetings with management presentations. Direct portal access to key documents and metrics.

Alignment Mechanisms

10% GP commitment creating €29.3M co-investment alongside LPs. Preferred return of 6% before GP participation in profits. Clawback provisions ensuring GP only profits after LP return objectives achieved.

Conflict Management

Written conflicts policy with pre-approval requirements for related-party transactions. Independent fairness opinions for any GP co-investment opportunities. Full disclosure of all service provider relationships.

Risk Controls

Investment committee approval required for all acquisitions and material decisions. Concentration limits preventing over-exposure to single assets. Documented investment policy with clear guidelines and restrictions.
